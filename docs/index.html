About Us

Pinoy Kitchen

We serve only the best and authentic Kapangpangan and Filipino dishes

Most Filipino food and dishes are a delectable blend of native and foreign cuisines that have evolved through the centuries. Historians narrate that a couple of hundred years ago, foreign traders and settlers, colonizers and missionaries brought with them their native recipes and unique style of cooking to the Philippines.
